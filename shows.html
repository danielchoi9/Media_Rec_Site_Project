Shows you should Definitely Watch!

How to Explore the Shows

Click on any show box below to reveal more information about the show. Each box contains a brief synopsis, a link to watch the show, and a sample video trailer.

Shogun by FX

Watch Now

In feudal Japan, an English sailor finds himself shipwrecked and his crew slaughtered. Rescued by a local lord, he is forced to learn the ways of the samurai and navigate the complex political landscape of Japan while trying to find a way back home.

The Boys by Prime Video

Watch Now

In a world where superheroes are revered as celebrities, a group of vigilantes known as "The Boys" embark on a mission to take down corrupt superheroes who abuse their powers and exploit the public. With dark humor and gritty action, the series explores themes of power, corruption, and morality.

The Bad Batch by Disney+

Watch Now

Set in the Star Wars universe, "The Bad Batch" follows a group of genetically enhanced clone troopers who possess unique skills and abilities. After the events of the Clone Wars, they embark on daring missions while navigating the aftermath of the conflict and facing new threats.

Kingdom by Netflix

Watch Now

In medieval Korea, a mysterious plague breaks out, turning people into flesh-eating monsters. Crown Prince Lee Chang, along with his allies, must uncover the truth behind the outbreak and save his kingdom from both the undead and political intrigue.

Invincible by Prime Video

Watch Now

Mark Grayson, a seemingly ordinary teenager, discovers that he is the son of Omni-Man, the world's most powerful superhero. As he inherits his father's abilities, Mark must navigate the complexities of being a superhero while uncovering dark secrets about his family's past.

The Last Kingdom by Netflix

Watch Now

Based on Bernard Cornwell's historical novels, "The Last Kingdom" tells the story of Uhtred of Bebbanburg, a Saxon nobleman who is captured and raised by Vikings. As he navigates the conflicts between Saxons and Danes during the Viking Age, Uhtred seeks to reclaim his birthright and find his true identity.
