Movies you should Definitely Watch!

How to Explore the Movies

Click on any movie box below to reveal more information about the movie. Each box contains a brief synopsis, a link to watch the movie, and a sample video trailer.

Dune: Part Two

Watch Now

Set in the distant future, "Dune Part 2" continues the epic saga of Paul Atreides as he navigates the treacherous politics and mystical forces of the desert planet Arrakis. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Paul must confront his destiny as the prophesied messiah while facing powerful adversaries and uncovering the secrets of the universe.

Barbie

Watch Now

"Barbie" follows the iconic doll on a whimsical adventure through the vibrant world of Dreamtopia. Joined by her friends, Barbie embarks on a journey filled with magic, friendship, and self-discovery as she learns valuable lessons about courage, kindness, and following your dreams.

Captain Phillips

Watch Now

Based on a true story, "Captain Phillips" recounts the harrowing ordeal of Captain Richard Phillips and his crew when their cargo ship is hijacked by Somali pirates. As tensions escalate and lives hang in the balance, Phillips must use all his wits and resourcefulness to survive and protect his crew from the ruthless pirates.

Interstellar

Watch Now

In a future where Earth is becoming uninhabitable, "Interstellar" follows a team of astronauts who embark on a daring mission through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. As they journey across the cosmos, they must confront the mysteries of space and time while grappling with the personal sacrifices required for the survival of the human race.

John Wick: Chapter 4

Watch Now

The fourth installment in the action-packed "John Wick" franchise, Chapter 4 continues the story of the legendary hitman as he finds himself on the run from an army of assassins. With enemies closing in from all sides, John Wick must rely on his unparalleled skills and determination to survive and seek vengeance against those who betrayed him.

Parasite

Watch Now

Winner of multiple Academy Awards including Best Picture, "Parasite" is a darkly comedic thriller that explores the class divide in modern society. When a poor family schemes their way into the lives of a wealthy household, their newfound prosperity quickly unravels into a twisted tale of greed, deception, and unexpected consequences.
